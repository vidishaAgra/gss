Founding Members

Muljibhai Patel

Welcome to Gujarati Samaj of Sacramento. I am lucky and blessed to have been one of the founding members and a President of the Samaj four times.

The Samaj has grown from a few families to many. I have worked with many wonderful youth and seniors over the years to elevate the Samaj and perform many activities such as kathas, nataks, picnics, Diwali shows, bus tours, and more. I look to a day when we can all pray in our Samaj temple and have activities in the Samaj hall.
